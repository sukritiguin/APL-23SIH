Revolutionizing Street Lighting: Cutting Costs, Enhancing Efficiency Traditional IoT-based street light installations come with a hefty price tag, often costing cities millions. However, our innovative solution redefines this paradigm, delivering the same benefits at nearly half the cost. Unlike conventional approaches that involve microcontroller boards for each lamp post and rely on technologies like GPS, LoRa, Zigbee, or Bluetooth, our approach minimizes complexity. By reducing the number of wires and centralizing control, we use a single Internet-enabled microcontroller, the Nodemcu, to manage multiple street lights (15-20 lights) in a zone. This not only significantly cuts installation costs but also ensures fault detection and intelligent illumination control based on motion detection. Our mission is to make advanced street lighting solutions accessible, cost-effective, and seamlessly integrated with modern urban infrastructure.
